Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about working with Global Website Studio.

What is your typical project timeline?

+

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but typically range from 4-12 weeks. A simple brochure website may take 4-6 weeks, while a complex e-commerce platform with multiple integrations could take 10-12 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients in different time zones?

+

Yes, we regularly work with clients across different time zones. We schedule meetings at mutually convenient times and use collaboration tools that allow for asynchronous communication. Our team is flexible and accustomed to working with international clients.

What is your pricing structure?

+

We offer custom pricing based on project requirements. After our initial consultation, we provide a detailed quote outlining costs. We offer both fixed-price projects for well-defined scopes and time-and-materials billing for more flexible projects. Contact us for a personalized quote.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

+

Yes, we offer several maintenance packages to keep your website secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. Our support plans include regular updates, security monitoring, backups, and technical support. We'll discuss maintenance options during the project planning phase.
