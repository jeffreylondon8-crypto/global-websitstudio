How I Create Websites

My proven 5-step process ensures your website meets your business goals and provides an excellent experience for your international customers.

1

Discovery & Strategy

I begin by understanding your business, target audience, and goals. We'll discuss your brand, services, and what makes your business unique.

2

Planning & Design

I create a sitemap and design mockups for your approval. This includes layout, color schemes, typography, and user experience planning.

3

Development

Using modern web technologies, I build a responsive, fast-loading website that works perfectly on all devices and browsers.

4

Content & Testing

I help you prepare content and test the website thoroughly for functionality, performance, and compatibility across different regions.

5

Launch & Support

After your approval, I launch your website and provide training on how to manage it. Ongoing support ensures everything runs smoothly.
