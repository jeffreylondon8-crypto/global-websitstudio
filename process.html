Our Proven Process

We follow a meticulous 5-step methodology to ensure your website not only meets but exceeds your business objectives. Transparency and collaboration are at the heart of our approach.

The Global Website Studio Process

Our structured approach ensures every project is delivered on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards.

1

Discovery & Strategy

1-2 Weeks

We begin with an in-depth analysis of your business, target audience, and competitive landscape. This phase includes stakeholder interviews, market research, and goal setting to establish a solid foundation for your project.

Business Requirements Analysis
Target Audience Research
Competitive Analysis
Project Scope Definition
2

Design & Prototyping

2-3 Weeks

Our design team creates wireframes and interactive prototypes based on the strategy phase. We focus on user experience, visual design, and brand alignment, with regular reviews and revisions based on your feedback.

Wireframe Creation
UI/UX Design
Interactive Prototypes
Design Review Sessions
3

Development & Integration

3-5 Weeks

Using modern technologies and best practices, we build a robust, scalable website. This phase includes front-end and back-end development, third-party integrations, and content management system implementation.

Front-end Development
Back-end Development
Third-party Integrations
CMS Implementation
4

Testing & Quality Assurance

1-2 Weeks

We conduct comprehensive testing across devices, browsers, and regions to ensure flawless performance. This includes functionality testing, performance optimization, security audits, and user acceptance testing.

Cross-browser Testing
Mobile Responsiveness
Performance Optimization
Security Audits
5

Launch & Ongoing Support

Ongoing

We manage the deployment process, provide training, and offer ongoing support. Our relationship continues post-launch with maintenance, updates, and analytics to ensure your website continues to perform optimally.

Deployment & Launch
Training & Documentation
Ongoing Maintenance
Performance Analytics
